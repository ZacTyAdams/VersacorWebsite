EXECUTIVE TEAM

 

The Executive Team of Versacor is a unique blend of talent and experience from the pest management industry.  Averaging over 20 years of industry experience in service, sales and operations, they are dedicated to providing the highest quality service to commercial clients.

 

JASON EICHER | PRESIDENT

 

Jason Eicher, President, is one of the founders of Versacor Enterprises, LLC and a 16 year pest management industry veteran.  Jason’s vision of Versacor led to the establishment of the Leadership Principles of the company.  These are encompassed in the mission statement and core values of being recognized as a premium service company devoted to quality, environmental stewardship and an unwavering commitment to our employees and clients.

 

As President of Versacor, Jason oversees the daily operations of the company and takes the lead role in setting the standard for service quality.  He believes that flawless execution is vital to the successful delivery of Versacor’s services and consistency is crucial to on-going customer satisfaction.  Through his leadership, Versacor Enterprises LLC is already being recognized as one of the fastest growing pest management companies in Texas.

 

Before founding Versacor, Jason was a regional vice president for The Steritech Group, Inc. and oversaw a 17-state region.  He started in the pest management industry in 1997 running branch operations before assuming responsibility for a multi-state operation.  Jason’s passion for technology led to company-wide improvements in quality and organizational efficiencies.  During his tenure, his operations were consistently ranked at the top of the organization. Prior to entering the pest management field, Jason worked for Duquesne Light Company as an engineer.  He is a graduate of Grove City College where he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Metallurgical Engineering.  He resides in Southlake, Texas with his wife, Ana.

RETURN TO TOP

 

 

ERIC EICHER | FOUNDER AND DIRECTOR

 

Eric Eicher is a founder and director of Versacor Enterprises, LLC.  Alongside brothers Jason, Bryan, and Jeff, Eric helped found Versacor as a commercial-only Managed Pest Solutions company.

 

Prior to founding Versacor, Eric was one of the founders of The Steritech Group, Inc. (1986) and in his last position was responsible for mergers and acquisitions. Prior to this role, Eric was the President and Chief Operating Officer of Steritech’s Pest Prevention Division, a position in which he was responsible for the successful operation of 28 branch offices around the country.

 

In addition to a 27 year career in the pest management industry, Eric is a published author, having penned articles for trade publications such as Food Quality Magazine, Food Industry Advisor, Today’s Facility Manager and Executive Housekeeping Today and has lent his expertise to dozens of others.   He has also spoken at numerous industry events.  In 2004, Eric was presented with Pest Control Technology Magazine & Syngenta Professional’s Leadership Award. Eric has also worked in the National Pest Management Association (NPMA), where he was appointed to the Board of Directors and served for two years on the Commercial Committee, the last year as Chairman of the committee.  Eric holds a Bachelor of Science in Biology from Grove City College.  A native of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Eric now resides in Grapevine, Texas.  He has a wife, Cindy, and three boys.

RETURN TO TOP

 

 

STEVE BYRD | GENERAL MANAGER, DFW

 

Steve Byrd is responsible for operating the Dallas/ Fort Worth branch.  As General Manager, Steve oversees all aspects of sales, operations, account management and specific marketing functions for the company.

 

Steve Byrd has over 18 years experience in the pest management industry, most recently with Myers Pest and Termite Services as Operations Director.  Prior to that he worked with Home Team Pest Defense as an Operations Director and spent 10 years with Greyhound Lines as Corporate Operations Manager.  During his career, Steve has worked in numerous positions including sales, marketing, management, business development and as a client retention specialist.

 

Over the years Steve has acquired a reputation as being both customer and quality oriented.  He has mentored literally hundreds of industry associates and has been instrumental in the rapid rise of some of the most successful companies in the pest management industry.  Steve is a native of Louisiana who has lived in Texas for over 40 years.  He currently resides in Bedford, Texas with his wife and 3 daughters.

RETURN TO TOP

 

 

BRYAN EICHER | CONTROLLER

 

Bryan Eicher is responsible for all financial and operational accounting functions for Versacor Enterprises, LLC.  As the Controller of Versacor, Bryan develops and maintains financial controls and oversees accounting activities for the company including financial statement preparation, accounts payable, payroll and compliance issues.  He is also involved in procurement, insurance and human resources.

 

Bryan has almost 30 years experience in the pest management industry and is one of the founders of Versacor.  Prior to working with Versacor, Bryan served in several different accounting and finance positions with Rentokil Initial plc during a successful 25-year tenure.  His last position with Rentokil Initial was as Financial Reporting Manager.   He was also involved with auditing for mergers and acquisitions, general staff accounting and preparation of financial statements.

 

Bryan is a graduate of Grove City College with a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration, Accounting concentration and resides in Keller, Texas.

RETURN TO TOP

 

 

RAYMOND THOMPSON, PH.D. | TECHNICAL ADVISOR

 

Dr. Thompson is a Technical Advisor for Versacor Enterprises, LLC.  He is responsible for various aspects of the technical side of the business revolving around training, quality assurance and licensing/certification.

 

Dr. Thompson has over 40 years experience in pest management or pest management related fields.  He has been an Assistant Professor of Extension Entomology, a Director of R & D with Biological Control Company, an owner of Dr. T’s Pest Control, a Technical Specialist and Training Director with Terminix International and a Consultant with Innovative Pest Management Associates, Inc.

 

Dr. Thompson graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Zoology from Eastern New Mexico University and obtained his Masters degree and Ph.D. degree from the University of Arkansas in Entomology.

RETURN TO TOP

 

JEFFREY D. EICHER, JD, CPA | OWNER/ADVISOR

 

Jeff Eicher is one of the owners of Versacor Enterprises, LLC.   He advises the company on legal, financial, tax and accounting matters.  Jeff is a Certified Public Accountant and attorney licensed to practice in Pennsylvania, Federal District Court and the U.S. Tax Court.  He has worked for both a Certified Public Accounting firm and a Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania based law firm.  He has been employed for the past 28 years as a Professor of Finance at Clarion University of Pennsylvania.  In addition to his teaching duties, he serves as Chairperson of the Department of Finance, Director of the Center for Legal Education, and Director of the Personal Finance Program.  He has also been engaged in the private practice of tax planning and advising since 1983. Jeff graduated from Clarion State College of Pennsylvania in 1977 with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, Accounting major, and from the University of Pittsburgh School of Law in 1980.  He resides in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania with his wife, Susan, and two children.
