ECO-FRIENDLY PEST SOLUTIONS

 

​

 

Versacor’s EcoGuard® service eliminates pests in an environmentally friendly way, avoiding unnecessary pesticide usage while delivering exceptional results.

 

A healthy school environment, free from pests and pesticide exposure, is essential. Children and young adults are especially susceptible to pesticide exposure and the allergens that come with certain types of pest infestations. This makes eliminating pests in a school environment a particularly challenging task. At Versacor, our exclusive environmentally friendly programs are up to the challenge.

 

MANAGED PEST SOLUTIONS

 

A Managed Pest Solution protects the health, property and reputation of our clients. We begin by totally eliminating all existing pest activity and implementing a proactive program that monitors for new activity. When pest activity is found, we eliminate it using our environmentally friendly EcoGuard® service. Preventive measures, such as caulking and sealing, are then taken to prevent a reoccurrence. Most importantly, your account is actively managed by our Quality Management team to ensure quality, consistency, and long-term results.

 

ECOGUARD® SERVICE

 

Versacor’s EcoGuard® service eliminates pests in an environmentally friendly way, avoiding unnecessary pesticide usage while delivering exceptional results. Our methods meet the USDA’s Cooperative State Research, Education, and Extension Service (CSREES) and U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s (EPA) strategic plan for implementing IPM practices in schools. As a leader in innovative green practices, we minimize the risks of pesticide contamination and provide a cleaner, healthier environment for both staff and students.

 

COMMUNICATION

 

Communication is a vital component of every Versacor pest program. From scheduling each service visit in advance, to checking in and checking out before and after service, your Versacor Service Specialist ensures an open line of communication. In addition, the results of each service are detailed on a service report along with structural, storage and sanitation recommendations, and a service logbook is maintained containing full documentation of your customized service program.

 

RESPONSIVENESS

 

A pest sighting in a school building is a serious issue, requiring quick action. That’s why we’ve developed an industry-leading response plan for schools: we respond to requests for emergency service within 30 minutes and a site visit is made the same day, or in accordance to the urgency of the situation. And of course, emergency service is provided 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.
