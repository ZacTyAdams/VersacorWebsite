PROTECTING HARD EARNED REPUTATIONS

 

​

 

You’ve worked hard to build and protect your reputation.  You’ve created a competitive advantage through unparalleled service and meticulous attention to detail. However, pest infestations of any kind can quickly tarnish a sterling reputation. This is where Versacor™ comes in.  Our exclusive Managed Pest Solution program protects your hard earned reputation and delivers results vital to guest satisfaction.

 

ECOGUARD® SERVICE

 

Versacor’s EcoGuard® service is a safer, more effective way to protect your business, eliminating pests while avoiding unnecessary pesticide usage.  This environmentally friendly approach is consistent with the mission of organizations like the Green Hotels Association, which promotes and supports the “Greening” of the lodging industry.  And because our EcoGuard® service results in little or no pesticide exposure, you, your staff and your guests enjoy a healthier environment. It not only makes sense, it’s the right thing to do.

 

MANAGED PEST SOLUTION

 

A Managed Pest Solution protects the health, property and reputation of our clients.  We begin by totally eliminating all existing pest activity and implementing a proactive program that monitors for new activity. When pest activity is found, we eliminate it using our environmentally friendly EcoGuard® service.  Preventive measures, such as caulking and sealing, are then taken to prevent a reoccurrence. Most importantly, your account is actively managed by our Quality Management team to ensure quality, consistency, and long-term results. GUARANTEED PEST ELIMINATION

 

Protecting your business and reputation is what we’re all about.  Because we understand the importance of a pest-free environment, we offer a powerful guarantee that puts our reputation on the line.  Our Total Protection Guarantee gives you the security of knowing that we’re as committed to your business as you are.

 

COMMUNICATION

 

Communication is a vital component of every Versacor pest program. From scheduling each service visit in advance to checking in and checking out before and after service, your Versacor Service Specialist ensures an open line of communication. In addition, the results of each service are detailed on a service report along with structural, storage and sanitation recommendations, and a service logbook is maintained containing full documentation of your customized service program.

 

RESPONSIVENESS

 

A pest sighting in a hotel or resort requires quick action. That is why at Versacor, we respond 24 hours per day, 7 days per week. If you have an emergency service request, we’ll respond within 30 minutes, then make a site visit the same day, or in accordance to the urgency of the situation.
