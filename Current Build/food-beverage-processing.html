FOOD AND BEVERAGE PROCESSING

GUARANTEED COMPLIANCE AND DOCUMENTATION

 

​

 

Versacor’s AuditPro™ Managed Pest Programs have set a new standard for compliance, documentation and reporting for the food and beverage industry.

 

Growing concern about the world’s food supply has fueled increased scrutiny of the food and beverage industry. Today’s food producers must be ever vigilant to meet the requirements of regulators, third-party auditors and their own missions. This is where Versacor can help. Through our AuditPro® Managed Pest Programs, we are setting a new standard for compliance, documentation and reporting for the food and beverage industry. Environmental sustainability is also important. Our environmentally friendly EcoGuard® service eliminates pests while reducing pesticide usage and delivering exceptional results. This philosophy coincides with The Grocery Manufacturers Association’s mission of helping members initiate environmentally friendly business practices.

 

AUDITPRO® MANAGED PEST PROGRAMS

 

Versacor’s AuditPro® Managed Pest Programs are designed to consistently deliver high-quality pest services that exceed the requirements of all government regulatory authorities and third-party auditors. Each customized program specifically meets the needs of the facility for which it was designed, including the required standards for documentation and reporting. Versacor’s AuditPro® Program was specifically designed to be compliant with the certification requirements of the Safe Quality Food (SQF) and British Retail Consortium (BRC) programs as outlined in the Global Food Safety Initiative.

 

AUDITPRO® CERTIFIED SPECIALISTS

 

Your AuditPro® service is delivered by one of Versacor’s AuditPro® Certified Specialists. Each Certified Specialist must meet stringent requirements for performance, experience and training to achieve the AuditPro® Certified designation. These include completion of our own AuditPro® Food Plant Training Program as well as certification by the National Pest Management Association (NPMA) in Pest Management Standards for Food Plants and the American Institute of Baking (AIB) Food Safety Essentials program.

 

AUDITPRO® GUARANTEE

 

Protecting your business and reputation is what we’re all about. Because we understand the importance of regulatory compliance as well as a pest-free environment, we offer a powerful guarantee that puts our reputation on the line. Our AuditPro™ Guarantee gives you the security of knowing that we are as committed to your business as you are.

 

DOCUMENTATION & REPORTING

 

Documentation is a critical component of an AuditPro® Managed Pest Program. Our documentation and reporting package will satisfy all regulatory agencies and third-party auditors.  The results of each service are detailed on a service report along with structural, storage and sanitation recommendations.  A service logbook is also maintained that contains full documentation of the service program, including emergency numbers, service specifications, licenses and insurance, a monitoring log/ trend report, and a complete set of labels and material safety data sheets in electronic or hard-copy format.

 

RESPONSIVENESS

 

We know how important it is to our clients to receive an immediate response to pest sightings. That’s why we’ve developed an industry-leading response plan for food and beverage processing facilities: we respond to emergency service requests within 30 minutes and arrive on site within 2 hours or in accordance to the urgency of the situation.  And of course, emergency service is provided 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.
