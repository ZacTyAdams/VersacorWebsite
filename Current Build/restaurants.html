RESTAURANTS

SOLUTIONS VITAL TO GUEST SATISFACTION

 

 

​

Versacor protects hard earned reputations and delivers the results vital to guest satisfaction and increased guest loyalty.

 

At Versacor, we recognize that in the highly competitive restaurant industry it is important to satisfy every guest, every time.  You work hard to exceed your customers’ expectations and create loyalty.  However, pest infestations of any kind can quickly damage a reputation that took years to build. Preventing that is where Versacor comes in.  Our exclusive Managed Pest Solution program protects your hard earned reputation and delivers the results vital to guest satisfaction and increased guest loyalty—both drivers of restaurant profitability.

 

MANAGED PEST SOLUTIONS

 

A Managed Pest Solution protects the health, property and reputation of our clients.  We begin by totally eliminating all existing pest activity and implementing a proactive program that monitors for new activity.  When pest activity is found, we eliminate it using our environmentally friendly EcoGuard® service. Preventive measures, such as caulking and sealing, are then taken to prevent a reoccurrence.  Most importantly, your account is actively managed by our Quality Management team to ensure quality, consistency, and long-term results.

 

GUARANTEED PEST ELIMINATION

 

Protecting your business and reputation is what we’re all about.  Because we understand the importance of a pest-free environment, we offer a powerful guarantee that puts our reputation on the line.  Our Total Protection Guarantee™ gives you the security of knowing that we’re as committed to your business as you are.  Environmental sustainability is also important.  Our EcoGuard® service eliminates pests in an environmentally friendly way, avoiding unnecessary pesticide usage while delivering exceptional results.  These methods coincide with the National Restaurant Association’s Conserve: Solutions for Sustainability and their initiative, Greener Restaurants.  As a leader in innovative green practices, Versacor minimizes the risks of pesticide contamination and provides a cleaner, healthier environment for you, your guests and your employees.

 

COMMUNICATION

 

Communication is a vital component of every Versacor pest program.  From scheduling each service visit in advance, to checking in and checking out before and after service, your Versacor Service Specialist ensures an open line of communication.  In addition, the results of each service are detailed on a service report along with structural, storage and sanitation recommendations, and a service logbook is maintained containing full documentation of your customized service program.

 

RESPONSIVENESS

 

A pest sighting in a restaurant requires quick action.  That is why at Versacor, we respond 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.  If you have an emergency service request, we’ll respond within 30 minutes, and a site visit will be made the same day, or in accordance to the urgency of the situation.
