ENHANCING WORKPLACE HEALTH AND SAFETY

 

​

 

The prevention of pests in a manufacturing environment is an integral part of an overall safety and sanitation program.

 

When it comes to manufacturing there can be no tolerance of pests.  Manufacturing companies must be constantly vigilant to meet the standards of their customers and provide a safe, hygienic workplace for employees.  The prevention of pests in a manufacturing environment is an integral part of an overall safety and sanitation program.  This is where Versacor can help.  Through our Managed Pest Solution program we’re setting a new standard for compliance, documentation and reporting for manufacturing facilities of all types.

 

MANAGED PEST SOLUTIONS

 

A Managed Pest Solution is designed to protect the health, property and reputation of our clients. We begin by totally eliminating existing pest activity.  Then we set up a proactive program to monitor for newly introduced pests.  If activity is found, we eliminate it using our environmentally friendly EcoGuard® service. Preventive measures, such as caulking and sealing, are then taken to prevent a reoccurrence.  Most importantly, your account is actively managed by our Quality Management team to ensure quality, consistency, and long-term results.

 

DOCUMENTATION & REPORTING

 

Documentation is a critical component of a Managed Pest Program.  Our documentation and reporting package will satisfy all regulatory agencies and third-party auditors—guaranteed.  Results of each service are detailed on a service report as well as structural, storage and sanitation recommendations.  A service logbook contains full documentation of your customized service program, including device-level detail such as monitoring logs and other required reports.

 

RESPONSIVENESS

 

A pest sighting in a manufacturing facility requires quick action. That is why at Versacor, we respond 24 hours per day, 7 days per week.  If you have an emergency service request, we’ll respond within 30 minutes, and make a site visit the same day, or in accordance to the urgency of the situation.
