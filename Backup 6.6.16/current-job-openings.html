CURRENT JOB OPENINGS

 

Service Specialist - Houston / San Antonio / Wichita Falls

 

Versacor Enterprises, LLC is a rapidly growing Texas-based company providing Managed Pest Solutions for commercial and industrial businesses.  Our customers span a wide range of industries that require the very highest standards in pest management: food and beverage processing, warehousing, hospitality, restaurant, health care, schools, pharmaceutical and property management.  We were founded on the principle that our clients deserve quality and value in their pest service, delivered by experts who share a passion for achieving unparalleled results.


Consistently delivering exceptional service is the standard that drives and motivates the people at Versacor. We are committed to environmentally friendly pest management solutions.

 

Our vision is to build a world-class company known for quality, value and innovation and to always operate in a way that safeguards the environment.  We believe that the key to our success is in hiring only the best people who share our passion and will contribute to an environment of teamwork, camaraderie and mutual respect.


This is an excellent opportunity for someone who prefers to be out of the office environment and in the field in a company provided service vehicle in a flexible, independent work environment.

 

We provide 2 weeks dedicated classroom training in the Dallas-Fort Worth Home Office, followed by four weeks field training in the Houston, San Antonio or Wichita Falls based route.


Versacor is seeking a professional, hardworking service specialist who will be responsible for:

  • Providing high quality pest solutions to commercial clients.
  • Organizing and managing a service route of these accounts.
  • Communicating recommendations and interacting with clients as a partner in providing a pest free environment.


You can expect to get out of this position what you put into it with a compensation plan that includes base pay plus sales and production commissions.

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

Some of the personal attributes found in others who have been successful in performing this type of work include:

  • Unwavering professionalism
  • Good speaking and listening skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Energetic and friendly demeanor
  • Enjoys working with hands
  • Sales minded a plus
  • Prior pest experience is preferred, but not required


REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must have some computer knowledge.
  • Must have a valid driver’s license and good driving record.  This position involves driving to each account.
  • Must be able to work required hours including willingness to be on-call during off work hours and weekends as necessary.
  • Prior experience in the service industry is a plus.
  • Must pass a pre-employment background check and drug test.


COMPENSATION/BENEFITS:

Versacor offers an excellent compensation and benefits package including base salary and production commission, health/life/disability insurance, 401K plan, paid vacation, and a company vehicle for company use.


Our pledge is to provide all employees with opportunities for professional development and career advancement.  Versacor is an equal opportunity employer and welcomes people of all races, nationalities and ethnicities.

 

Immigration sponsorship is not available for this role.
